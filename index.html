What wildlife are we expected to see in Jordan?

Jordan is home to vast wildlife. From migratory birds to Arabian oryx, red foxes, Nubian ibex, and the rich marine life of the Red Sea. Rare sightings include striped hyenas, sand cats, Eurasian badgers, and whale sharks.
