What wildlife are we expected to see in Jordan?

Jordan is home to vast wildlife. The country is a very important migratory route for many bird species, the red sea is home to over 500 species of fish. Apart from the sea, Jordanian wilderness is a scenic adventure, yet to be witnessed by many. The chances of spotting Arabian oryx, water buffalo, red foxes, rock hyrax, desert foxes & Nubian ibex. There are many times few people have recorded in Jordan are extremely rare to spot. Just when many say this and shun nature such as striped hyenas, sand cats, Eurasian badgers and whale sharks. With some great luck you could see one of the very few people that were able to spot them!
